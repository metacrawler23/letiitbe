1. THE SPORTSCASH PROGRAM

What is the SportsCash program?

SportsCash is one of the major benefits of being a member of SportsPass. It is real cash rewards you earn that can offset the costs of playing sport by a lot not a little. At the same time, SportsCash supports your club directly by providing an ongoing stream of funds from you – so club members become silent sponsors .

You earn SportsCash from your shopping simply, automatically, at no extra cost to you. Just do the shopping you are going to do at our participating retailers. Every time you buy, you earn SportsCash worth up to 30% of your spend. From your everyday shopping like groceries and petrol. And from your online shopping.

And you’ll get Hot Deals sent to you regularly where you can earn more SportsCash. 

Everything happens behind the scenes seamlessly and automatically. You shop, your SportsCash accumulates and your donations go to your club.

SportsCash does not cost you any more – it’s the commissions our retailers are happy to provide for your business. 

 

What is SportsCash?

SportsCash is exactly as it says – extra cash that you earn by shopping at our participating outlets.  At no extra cost to you – they are the commissions our retailers are happy to provide for your business. To see how much SportsCash you could earn, check out the calculator at the website. You'll be surprised.

Your SportsCash rewards you and your club at the same time. When you join, you nominate what proportion of your SportsCash you want to keep for your use – and what you wish to contribute to your club.

3. SPORTSCASH

How do I earn SportsCash?

You earn SportsCash by buying from our participating retailers in 3 ways

1. By buying from the Online Shopping Centre which features over 300 online retailers

2. By buying from the Factory Direct where there are thousands of products to choose from

3. By buying eVouchers for specific retailers. These eVouchers are emailed to you, and then you can redeem them in store.

The SportsCash you earn is credited to your account depending on when the funds are received from the retailers.

How much SportsCash can I earn?

It varies depending on the retailer – up to 30% with some retailers.

How can I check my balance and my transaction history?

You can check your balance and transaction history at any time at the SportsCash website. Click on the ‘My Profile’ button and follow the prompts to your very own account. You will need to enter your login details as this is a secure site.

4. SPORTSCASH eVOUCHERS

What is an eVoucher?

An eVoucher is similar to a gift card. You buy them at our website and the eVoucher is then sent to you by email. Simply print off the eVoucher store it on your phone. Then present to the retailer. 

How much SportsCash can you earn with eVouchers?

You earn SportsCash each time you purchase an eVoucher. The dollars earned depend on the retailer. Check out the range of retailers and the SportsCash amount at our website.

How do you buy an eVoucher?

Go to the eVouchers section at our website. Select the eVoucher you want to purchase. Depending on which eVoucher you choose, you will be directed to either

- a direct ordering page or

- to the retailers online store where you will need to follow the prompts

You can then choose the amount of the eVoucher and the number of eVouchers you want to order.

When will you receive your eVoucher?

Normally within one hour. But under some circumstances it may be delayed until the next business day.

Is there an expiry date on my eVoucher?

Yes depending on the eVoucher. The expiry date will be prominently displayed on the eVoucher itself.

Is there a cost to order an eVoucher?

It varies by retailer and on the way you pay. In most cases, there is a no cost option

5. SPORTSCASH ONLINE SHOPPING CENTRE

What is the SportsCash Online Shopping Centre?

The Online Shopping Centre offers you a wide range of online shopping stores to choose from. There are over 300 brands with 3+ million products.

By shopping via our Online Shopping Centre, you earn SportsCash with each purchase. So if your favourite online shop is part of our network, it makes sense to shop via us because you earn SportsCash you wouldn’t normally receive. 

How does the SportsCash Online Shopping Centre work?

Simply browse through our retailers and find where you would like to shop. Click on the logo and you will be then linked directly to the store site. Purchase the item(s) you want.

You can also use our Search & Compare tool to search by specific product. We will then identify the retailers who have that product and display them in order to fast track and streamline your shopping experience.

Each purchase will earn you SportsCash which will be credited to your account as soon as the funds are received from the store.

Can I shop by product?

Yes. By using Search & Compare - a powerful tool designed to simplify your online shopping with us. It will change the way you shop online.

Instead of choosing a retailer, you choose a type of product. Say jeans in womens fashion. Search & Compare then sorts through millions of products to display only those that meet your criteria. You will see not only the products but also the key details such as the name of the retailer, the price, the rewards you can earn.

You can then browse or refine and short list. And either add to your Wishlist or select the product you want and click to buy. 

When will I get my SportsCash?

SportsCash will be credited to your account as soon as we receive it from the retailer. Each retailer has different timeframes on when they pay this cash back to SportsCash. It could take up to 90 days be credited to your account depending on the returns policy of each retailer

What if I haven’t received my SportsCash?

Go to Contact Us. Select Shop – Missing Transaction. As part of your message, you need to provide us with the store name, the transaction date and amount plus the order number which you will find on your receipt from the retailer.  We will then contact the retailer and come back to you.

Do I get SportsCash with Hot Deals?

Yes. You get both the deal and the rewards

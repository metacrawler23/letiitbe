Create a SportsPass Account

To be able to add a membership card to your wallet, you first need to create a SportsPass account.

The SportsPass Account owner can then add multiple cards from multiple members all under their SportsPass account.

E.g. Parents & Guardians adding dependants to the same account for easy card retrieve.

Once you have created a new Sportspass account, please use Login.
